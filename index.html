Tips & Safety Guidelines
Science-backed advice for sustainable, healthy weight loss.
🏃 Exercise Plan
  • Wk 1–2: 20-min brisk walk daily
  • Wk 3–4: Add 3× bodyweight circuits
  • Wk 5–8: 40-min walk + 3× strength
  • Wk 9–13: Jog + 3× full-body workouts
  • 10-min walks after meals dramatically aid fat loss
  • Avoid exercising on a completely empty stomach early on
😴 Sleep & Recovery
  • Sleep 7–8 hours every night — critical for fat-loss hormones
  • Sleep deprivation raises cortisol → fat retention
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • Sleep before midnight for optimal growth hormone
  • Dark, cool room (18–22°C) dramatically improves quality
  • Consistent wake-up time, even weekends
🧠 Stress Management
  • High stress → cortisol → belly fat accumulation
  • 10-min deep breathing (4-7-8 method) daily
  • Identify emotional eating triggers
  • 5-min walk when cravings strike — they pass in 10 min
  • Journal cravings to reduce binge episodes by 40%
  • Social support significantly boosts success rates
⚠️ Safety Rules
  • Never below 1,500 kcal/day without a doctor
  • Do NOT skip more than 1 meal per day
  • Avoid crash diets — they lose muscle, not fat
  • Losing >1.5 kg/week = mostly muscle/water loss
  • All 3 macros in every meal
  • Dizzy, hair loss, extreme fatigue → see a doctor immediately
⏰ Meal Timing & IF
  • Eat in a 10–12 hour window (8 AM–8 PM)
  • Largest meal at lunch — lightest at dinner
  • Dinner at least 3 hours before sleep
  • Optional 16:8 IF from Week 7+: 12 PM–8 PM only
  • Never fast >16 hrs when doing strength training
  • Black coffee & green tea allowed during fast
🍽️ Eating Habits
  • Chew 20–30 times per bite; satiety takes 20 min
  • Smaller plates (200–250mm) reduces intake by ~22%
  • Start lunch & dinner with water or salad
  • 1 cheat meal/week (not a cheat day)
  • Avoid liquid calories — juice, chai, sweet lassi
  • Meal prep on Sunday reduces poor choices all week
