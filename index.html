Tips & Safety Guidelines
Science-backed advice for sustainable, healthy weight loss.
🏃 Exercise Plan (Sedentary → Active)
  • Week 1–2: 20–min brisk walk daily
  • Week 3–4: Add 3× bodyweight circuits (squats, push-ups)
  • Week 5+: 45-min walk + 2× resistance sessions
  • Even 10-min walks after meals dramatically aid fat loss
  • Avoid exercising on an empty stomach initially
😴 Sleep & Recovery
  • Sleep 7–8 hours every night — essential for fat loss hormones
  • Sleep deprivation raises cortisol → increased hunger & fat retention
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • Sleep before midnight for optimal melatonin & growth hormone
  • Aim for consistent wake-up time, even weekends
🧠 Stress Management
  • High stress → high cortisol → belly fat accumulation
  • Try 10-min daily deep breathing or meditation
  • Avoid emotional/stress eating — identify triggers
  • Social support significantly improves weight loss success
  • Journaling cravings can reduce binge episodes by 40%
⚠️ Safety Rules — Never Break These
  • Never go below 1,500 kcal/day without medical supervision
  • Do NOT skip more than 1 meal per day
  • Avoid crash diets — they cause muscle loss, not fat loss
  • Losing >1.5 kg/week = likely muscle, not just fat
  • Always include all 3 macros in every meal
  • If dizzy, fatigued, or losing hair — consult a doctor
