Tips & Safety Guidelines
Science-backed advice for sustainable, healthy weight loss.
🏃 Exercise Plan (Sedentary → Active)
  • Week 1–2: 20-min brisk walk daily (morning preferred)
  • Week 3–4: Add 3× bodyweight circuits (squats, push-ups, lunges)
  • Week 5–6: 30-min walk + 2× resistance sessions/week
  • Week 7–10: 45-min walk + 3× strength training (home or gym)
  • Week 11–13: Jog 20 min + 3× full-body workouts
  • 10-min walks after each meal boost fat burning significantly
  • Avoid exercising on a completely empty stomach early on
😴 Sleep & Recovery
  • Sleep 7–8 hours every night — critical for fat-loss hormones
  • Sleep deprivation raises cortisol → increased hunger & fat retention
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed; use blue-light glasses if needed
  • Sleep before midnight for optimal melatonin & growth hormone release
  • Aim for consistent wake-up time, even weekends
  • Sleep quality matters as much as quantity — dark, cool room (18–22°C)
🧠 Stress Management
  • High stress → high cortisol → belly fat accumulation
  • Try 10-min daily deep breathing (4-7-8 method) or meditation
  • Avoid emotional/stress eating — identify your triggers
  • Social support significantly improves weight loss success rates
  • Journaling cravings can reduce binge episodes by 40%
  • Take a 5-min walk when cravings strike — they pass in 10 minutes
⚠️ Safety Rules — Never Break These
  • Never go below 1,500 kcal/day without medical supervision
  • Do NOT skip more than 1 meal per day
  • Avoid crash diets — they cause muscle loss, not fat loss
  • Losing >1.5 kg/week = likely muscle, water loss — not just fat
  • Always include all 3 macros in every meal
  • If dizzy, fatigued, hair loss, or very cold — consult a doctor immediately
  • Take progress photos every 2 weeks — scale isn't everything
⏰ Meal Timing & Intermittent Fasting
  • Eat within a 10–12 hour window (e.g. 8 AM to 8 PM)
  • Breakfast: within 1 hour of waking up; jumpstarts metabolism
  • Largest meal at lunch — lightest at dinner (before 8 PM)
  • Gap of at least 3 hours between dinner and sleep
  • Optional 16:8 IF from Week 7+: eat 12 PM–8 PM (skip early breakfast)
  • Never fast for more than 16 hours when exercising
  • Black coffee/green tea allowed during fasting window
🍽️ Eating Habits & Cheat Meals
  • Eat slowly — chew 20–30 times per bite; satiety takes 20 min
  • Use smaller plates (200–250mm) — reduces intake by 22% on average
  • Start every lunch and dinner with a large glass of water or salad
  • Allow 1 cheat meal per week (not a cheat day) — prevents burnout
  • Cheat meal: Saturday lunch is ideal (most active socially)
  • Avoid liquid calories — juice, chai with sugar, lassi (unless planned)
  • Cook in bulk on Sundays — meal prep reduces poor food choices
